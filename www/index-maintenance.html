3-roll-2

A style of suit with 3 buttons on the front where the top button hole and button are on the lapel roll making it functionally equivalent to a 2-button suit.
Like the bottom button, the top button in a 3-roll-2 serves no functional purpose and very little aesthetic purpose yet it persists as an interesting stylistic option for those who care.
